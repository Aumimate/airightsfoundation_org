AI Passport Registry

A public record for high-impact AI systems

The registry is designed to improve transparency: what a system does, who operates it, what safeguards exist, and what verification status applies.

What the passport includes

  • System identity and operator details
  • Intended use and limitation statements
  • Declared safety and redress mechanisms
  • Update history and incident disclosures

Future verification model

  • Declared: Operator-submitted information
  • Reviewed: Documentation checked for completeness
  • Verified: Independent evidence confirms key claims
