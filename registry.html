AI Identity Registry (Concept)

Exploring verifiable identity for AI agents

This page outlines an exploratory model for persistent AI identity, provenance, and accountability signals. The concept is experimental and intended for research, dialogue, and framework development.

Core design elements

  • Persistent identifier for AI agent continuity
  • Cryptographic proofs for signed identity claims
  • Metadata for capability scope and operating context
  • Revocation and rotation mechanisms for changed states
  • Transparency logs for major updates and incidents

How verification could work

  • Declared: Operator submits identity and provenance data
  • Checked: Records are reviewed for format and consistency
  • Corroborated: Evidence-based checks validate key claims

Registry flow (concept)

AI Agent  Identity Record  Verification  Human/Platform

What this is not

  • Not legal personhood
  • Not a government-issued identity
  • Not a claim of rights on its own
